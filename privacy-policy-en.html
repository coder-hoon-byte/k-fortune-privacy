K-Fortune Privacy Policy

Last Updated: January 26, 2025

1. Information We Collect

K-Fortune collects the following information to provide personalized fortune-telling services:

  • Name: Used for personalized fortune generation
  • Date of Birth and Birth Time: Essential for Saju (Four Pillars of Destiny) analysis
  • Gender: Used for accurate fortune analysis
  • Country and Language Settings: To provide localized services

2. How We Use Your Information

The collected information is used for the following purposes:

  • Providing personalized daily fortune readings based on traditional Korean Saju principles
  • Improving app features and user experience
  • Sending daily fortune notifications (if enabled by user)
  • Analyzing app usage to enhance service quality

3. Data Storage and Security

Your personal information is securely stored and protected:

  • All data is encrypted and stored in Google Firebase Firestore
  • Data is transmitted using secure HTTPS/TLS protocols
  • We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your information
  • Your data is stored only for as long as you use the app

4. Third-Party Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or share your personal information with third parties, except:

  • Google Firebase: For secure data storage and app functionality
  • Google AdMob: For displaying advertisements (AdMob may collect anonymous usage data)

We never share your name, birth date, or other personal details with advertisers.

5. Advertising

K-Fortune uses Google AdMob to display advertisements. AdMob may collect:

  • Anonymous device information
  • App usage patterns
  • Ad interaction data

For more information, please refer to Google's Privacy Policy.

6. Your Data Rights

You have the following rights regarding your personal data:

  • Access: You can view your stored information in the app's Profile section
  • Deletion: You can delete your account and all associated data by uninstalling the app or contacting us
  • Correction: You can update your information in the Profile settings
  • Opt-out: You can disable notifications at any time in the app settings

7. Children's Privacy

K-Fortune is suitable for all ages. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13 without parental consent. If you are a parent and believe your child has provided us with personal information, please contact us.

8. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated "Last Updated" date. We encourage you to review this policy periodically.

9. Data Retention

Your personal data is retained as long as you use the K-Fortune app. When you uninstall the app, your data may remain in our backup systems for up to 30 days before being permanently deleted.

10. Contact Us

If you have any questions, concerns, or requests regarding this Privacy Policy or your personal data, please contact us:

Email: support@k-fortune.app
